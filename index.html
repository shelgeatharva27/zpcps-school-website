About us

Establishment

ZPCPS Kanki Tanda, a primary school established by the government of Maharashtra in 1990, is located in Kanki Tanda, a small village in the Kinwat region of Nanded district. Situated close to the border of Telangana, this village is home to about 40 families. Despite its modest size, the school serves a total student population of 50, reflecting its vital role in the community.

Education

The school offers education from Class 1 to Class 5, with each class having its own dedicated classroom and a small playground in front. It follows the curriculum set by the Maharashtra education board for primary education. Teachers at ZPCPS Kanki Tanda also provide training for competitive exams such as Navodaya, ensuring students are well-prepared. The school fosters a joyful and dedicated environment where education is the primary focus, reflecting the strong bond between teachers and students.

Family

Students here are very comfortable with their teachers, making the school feel like a family where every student is guided toward a bright future. Everything possible is done to strengthen this family and ensure that every student who steps into this school has a bright future.
